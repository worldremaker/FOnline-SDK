FOnline AngleScript compiler. Usage:
ASCompiler script_name.fos
 [-client] (compile client scripts)
 [-mapper] (compile mapper scripts)
 [-p preprocessor_output.txt]
 [-d SOME_DEFINE]*
 [-run func_name]*
*can be used over and over again
